Expert Care for Peak Performance: Meet Mohamed Aden

With over a decade of elite-level experience, I provide a rare fusion of clinical sports therapy and high-performance personal training. My mission is simple: to help busy professionals and high-achievers eliminate pain and unlock their physical potential.

The Gold Standard of Therapy

I was trained in Toronto, Canada—one of the world's most rigorous environments for physical therapy—where I qualified to the highest standards as a Registered Massage Therapist (RMT). This clinical background allows me to go far beyond "relaxation."

I specialize in rehabilitating complex issues, including:

  • Repetitive Strain & Sports Injuries: Expert treatment for conditions like Tennis Elbow, Golfer's Elbow, and Rotator Cuff Impingements.
  • Postural Correction: Undoing the physical toll of high-stress, desk-based corporate roles.
